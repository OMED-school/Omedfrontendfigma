This guide explains how to use the OMED School Ideas Platform for students, teachers, and principals.

For Students

Creating an Account

  1. Click "Sign Up" on the login page
  2. Enter your email, name, and password
  3. Your account will be created with "student" role by default
  4. Verify your email address (if required)

Submitting an Idea

  1. Click the "+ New Idea" button on the home page
  2. Fill in the idea form:
    • Title: A short, descriptive title
    • Description: Detailed explanation of your idea
    • Category: Select appropriate category (e.g., Technology, Facilities, Academic)
  3. Click "Submit Idea"
  4. Your idea will appear in the public feed immediately

Voting on Ideas

  • Upvote: Click the ‚Üë button to support an idea
  • Downvote: Click the ‚Üì button if you disagree
  • You can change your vote at any time
  • Vote count shows total: upvotes minus downvotes

Commenting

  1. Click on an idea card to open details
  2. Scroll to the comments section
  3. Type your comment in the text box
  4. Click "Post Comment"
  5. Reply to existing comments by clicking "Reply"

Using Chat

  1. Click the chat icon in the navigation bar
  2. Select a user from your contacts or search for one
  3. Type your message and press Enter or click Send
  4. Messages appear in real-time

Tracking Your Ideas

  • View all your submitted ideas in "My Ideas" section
  • See current status: Pending, Under Review, Approved, or Rejected
  • Receive notifications when teachers review your ideas

For Teachers

Accessing Teacher Dashboard

  1. Your account must be assigned "teacher" role by an administrator
  2. Log in with your credentials
  3. Click "Teacher Dashboard" in navigation

Reviewing Student Ideas

  1. Browse pending ideas in the review queue
  2. Click on an idea to see full details
  3. Read the description and comments
  4. Decide on action:
    • Approve: Forward to principal for budget approval
    • Reject: Decline with feedback
    • Request Changes: Ask student for more information
  5. Add review notes (visible to principal)
  6. Click appropriate action button

Adding Review Notes

  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Explain why you're approving or rejecting
  • Suggest improvements if needed
  • Notes help principals make informed decisions

Communicating with Students

  • Use the chat feature to discuss ideas
  • Leave comments on idea submissions
  • Provide encouragement and guidance

For Principals

Accessing Principal Dashboard

  1. Your account must be assigned "principal" role
  2. Log in with your credentials
  3. Click "Principal Dashboard" in navigation

Reviewing Teacher-Approved Ideas

  1. View ideas that teachers have approved
  2. Read student description and teacher notes
  3. Consider feasibility, budget, and impact

Making Final Approval

  1. Click on an idea from the principal review queue
  2. Click "Approve with Budget" or "Reject"
  3. If approving, enter:
    • Budget: Allocated amount (e.g., "‚Ç¨5000")
    • Timeline: Implementation schedule (e.g., "Q1 2025")
    • Priority: Low, Medium, High, or Critical
  4. Add implementation notes
  5. Click "Approve" to finalize

Tracking Approved Ideas

  • View all approved ideas with budget allocation
  • Monitor implementation progress
  • Update status as work progresses
  • Communicate with teachers and students

Common Features

Profile Management

  1. Click your profile icon/name
  2. Select "Profile" from dropdown
  3. Update your information:
    • Display name
    • Bio
    • Profile picture (if enabled)
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Notifications

  • Bell icon shows new notifications
  • Notifications for:
    • Your idea was reviewed
    • Someone commented on your idea
    • New ideas in your categories of interest
    • New messages received

Search and Filters

  • Search: Find ideas by keyword
  • Category Filter: Show ideas from specific categories
  • Status Filter: Filter by approval status
  • Sort: By date, votes, or comments

Tips and Best Practices

For Students

  • Be specific and clear in your idea descriptions
  • Include why your idea would benefit the school
  • Consider feasibility and cost
  • Engage with comments and feedback
  • Support others' good ideas with upvotes

For Teachers

  • Review ideas promptly
  • Provide constructive feedback
  • Encourage student participation
  • Consider long-term impact
  • Forward promising ideas to administration

For Principals

  • Set realistic budgets
  • Prioritize high-impact ideas
  • Communicate decisions clearly
  • Follow through on approvals
  • Recognize student contributors

Troubleshooting

Can't Log In

  • Verify your email and password
  • Use "Forgot Password" to reset
  • Check if email verification is pending
  • Contact your administrator for role issues

Idea Not Submitting

  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check your internet connection
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Clear browser cache

Votes Not Counting

  • You must be logged in to vote
  • You can only vote once per idea
  • Refresh page to see updated counts

Messages Not Sending

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify recipient is a valid user
  • Try refreshing the chat
  • Log out and log back in

Getting Help

  • Contact your school's OMED administrator
  • Check the documentation homepage for guides
  • Report bugs on GitHub

Privacy and Safety

  • Your email is private and not shared
  • Your ideas are public within your school
  • Inappropriate content will be moderated
  • Report concerns to teachers or administrators
  • Be respectful in comments and chat
